Comparative Analysis of Balanced Diet Indices of different countries across various years and EAT-Lancet Recommendations

The website make a brief comparision of the average person's Balanced Diet Index (BDI), an index deviced by us, that takes into consideration the intake of Carbohydrates, Proteins(Vegetal and Animal), Fats, Sugar, Fruits amd Vegetables. These are measured against the recommended, EAT Lancet standard for the average person in that year. A "Scoring System" is introduced that measures the score of each country against the recommended healthy value of the diet. The scores are calculated out of 10 and are weighted by their percentage of healthy intake to get score of the country. This is a very close indication of the Balanced Diet followed in all the countries.

How to interact with the Visualization?

Upon Dragging the slider on the bottom of the map, year can be selected. For that selected year, Choropleth Map is displayed. Upon hovering on a country, the name and score is displayed. On clicking the country, the country's year wise analysis is displayed along with a Pie-Chart of the distribution of the diet with above mentioned nutritions. Upon using the slider again, the map, scatter line plot and the pie chart all update simultaneously.
